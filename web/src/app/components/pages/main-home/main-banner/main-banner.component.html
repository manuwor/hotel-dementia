YOU AREN’T IN THIS ALONE

     A community for people who are in pain and trying to escape reality through a game. This project will provide anonymity in the gameplay and chat room for people to express themselves and ignite hope together.

NEW WAY OF HEALING

     We believe the gameplay and NFT community can be the new way for better mental health. Partnering with mental healthcare professions, we will try our best to create a community of healing, hoping, and living.
